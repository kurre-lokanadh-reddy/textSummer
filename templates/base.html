About

Summerize.com UNDERSTANDING WANTS TO BE SIMPLE is an acadamic project done to help the understanding of summerizing of long documents ,in simple. Summerize focuses on providing the most efficient way of summerizing the given text. We used most of the well performing extractive methods along with a-few abstractive methods. The entire end-to-end project is developed in python (nltk,tensorflow,keras) ,flask is used in integration and html,css,js.
